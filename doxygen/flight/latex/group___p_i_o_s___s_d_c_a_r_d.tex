 \-This takes ca. 10 u\-S

\-If \-S\-D card was previously not available\-: \-Checks if the \-S\-D \-Card is available by sending the \-I\-D\-L\-E command at low speed.\par
 \-This takes ca. 500 u\-S!\par
 \-Once we got a positive response, \-S\-D\-C\-A\-R\-D\-\_\-\-Power\-On() will be called by this function to initialize the card completely.
